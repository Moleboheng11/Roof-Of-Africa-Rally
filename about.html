Experience the Legendary Roof of Africa Rally

The Roof of Africa Rally is one of the most famous off-road motorcycle races in the world. The event takes place in the mountains of Lesotho and attracts professional riders and adventure enthusiasts from many countries. The challenging terrain, steep hills, and beautiful landscapes make the rally one of the toughest races in global motorsport.

A Proud Motorsport Tradition Since 1967

The rally began in 1967 and has grown into an internationally recognised motorsport event. Every year riders travel to Lesotho to compete in the extreme mountain race. The event has become an important part of the country's sporting culture and attracts thousands of spectators.

An Event for Local and International Tourists

The Roof of Africa Rally attracts a wide range of tourists from both Lesotho and other countries. The event appeals to visitors who enjoy adventure, motorsports, and cultural experiences.
